Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

How long does delivery take?+
Delivery typically takes 2–5 business days depending on your location. We ship across India. You'll receive tracking updates via WhatsApp.
What is the shelf life of the spread?+
The spread has a shelf life of 4–6 weeks at room temperature and up to 3 months when refrigerated. Always use a clean, dry spoon to maximise freshness.
Is it safe for diabetics?+
Our spread contains zero added sugar and is sweetened only by dates and raw honey. Many of our diabetic customers enjoy it, but we always recommend consulting your doctor for personalised dietary advice.
Can I pay cash on delivery?+
Yes! We offer Cash on Delivery (COD), UPI (GPay, PhonePe), and Bank Transfer / NEFT. Choose your preferred payment method at checkout.
Do you offer bulk or corporate orders?+
Yes, we love bulk orders and gifting sets! Contact us via WhatsApp for corporate gifting, bulk pricing, and custom packaging options.
Is the product nut-allergy friendly?+
Our spread contains almonds and cashews, which are tree nuts. It is not suitable for anyone with nut allergies. Please read the ingredient list carefully before ordering.
